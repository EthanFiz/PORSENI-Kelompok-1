School Profile

Canisius College is a Jesuit school founded in a year 1927 and is located at Jl. Menteng Raya No.64,

Kota Jakarta Pusat and is an all boys school and is the only Jesuit school in Indonesia to also allow

Junior Highschool students other than Highschool students.

Academic Activities

MPLS & Ignatian Brotherhood

Is a yearly event held at the start of every academic year with the goal of introducing new students to the enviroment in Canisius College

Canisius College Cup

Is a yearly event which was previously known as POR CC, and is a sports event where every student becomes part of the commitee and have full control of the event.

Canisius Talent Spotting

Is a yearly event held by teachers with help from some students and hold compititions including sports, science, and more as a way to promote Cansius College to the next generation of students.
